What is Numerology?

📊

Ancient Wisdom

Numerology is an ancient divination practice that reveals the spiritual significance and vibration of numbers. It connects mathematical principles to spiritual insights, helping you understand your life's purpose and destiny.

🔢

Life Path Number

Your Life Path Number is calculated from your birth date and represents your life's journey, innate talents, and purpose. It reveals the path you're meant to walk and the lessons you'll learn.

📝

Name Number

Your Name Number, derived from your birth name using the Pythagorean system, reveals your character, abilities, and how you express yourself in the world. It's also called the Expression Number.

👑

Master Numbers

Master Numbers (11, 22, 33) carry heightened spiritual significance and are not reduced to single digits. They represent intuition, vision, and profound spiritual responsibility and potential.

🔤

Pythagorean System

This system assigns numbers 1-9 to letters A-Z based on their position in the alphabet. Each letter carries its own vibration, and combined they reveal the numerical essence of names and concepts.

✨

Self-Discovery

Numerology serves as a mirror for self-reflection and spiritual guidance. Understanding your numbers helps you align with your true purpose and navigate life's journey with greater clarity and confidence.
